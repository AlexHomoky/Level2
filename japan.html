Welcome to Japan


Japan is a unique country with a radically different history, culture and language from must of the rest of the world. For example, it was still a feudal society well into the middle of the 19th century. For this reason it's aesthetics and architecture is rather unique. You will often see medieval or older styles of building directly adjacent to 20th and 21st century structures. But the real surprise is, it works!

Anyone living the Digital Nomad lifestyle will certainly relish a year or two in Japan. We can arrange specialist visas and accommodations, countrywide excursions, language lessons, local guides, local contacts, and much more. Definately an adventure not to be missed!.
